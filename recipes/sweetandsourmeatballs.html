Sweet and Sour Meatballs

Description

These Sweet and Sour Meatballs are a delicious twist on a classic dish. Juicy meatballs are simmered in a tangy sauce made from vinegar, pineapple juice, and brown sugar — perfect for serving over rice or as an appetizer. Whether you’re hosting a party or just craving something flavorful and comforting, this recipe is quick to prepare and a definite crowd-pleaser.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup bread crumbs
  • 1/4 cup chopped onion
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/3 cup vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 cup pineapple juice

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix ground beef, egg, bread crumbs, onion, salt, and pepper.
  3. Form into small meatballs and place on a baking sheet.
  4. Bake meatballs for 20–25 minutes until browned and cooked through.
  5. In a saucepan, combine brown sugar, vinegar, soy sauce, cornstarch, and pineapple juice.
  6. Bring the sauce to a boil, stirring constantly until thickened.
  7. Add cooked meatballs to the sauce and simmer for 10 minutes.
  8. Serve warm over rice or as an appetizer.
