Sweet and Sour Meatballs

Description

These Sweet and Sour Meatballs are a delicious twist on a classic dish. Juicy meatballs are simmered in a tangy sauce made from vinegar, pineapple juice, and brown sugar — perfect for serving over rice or as an appetizer. Whether you’re hosting a party or just craving something flavorful and comforting, this recipe is quick to prepare and a definite crowd-pleaser.
