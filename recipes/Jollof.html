Jollof Rice

Description

Jollof Rice is a beloved West African dish made with rice, tomatoes, onions, peppers, and flavorful spices. It’s a one-pot meal that brings people together — bold in taste and vibrant in color.

Whether served with chicken, beef, or fish, Jollof Rice is perfect for celebrations, family dinners, and special gatherings. Its signature smoky flavor and rich aroma make it a classic favorite.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups long grain rice (washed)
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 large onion (chopped)
  • 1 can (400g) chopped tomatoes or blended fresh tomatoes
  • 3 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 red bell pepper (blended)
  • 2 cups chicken stock or water
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • 1 teaspoon curry powder
  • 1 bay leaf (optional)
  • Salt and seasoning to taste

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large pot. Add chopped onions and sauté until translucent.
  2. Add tomato paste and cook for a few minutes. Then add blended tomatoes and pepper.
  3. Cook the sauce until it reduces and darkens in color (about 15–20 minutes).
  4. Add thyme, curry, bay leaf, and seasoning. Stir well.
  5. Pour in the rice and chicken stock. Mix until rice is coated in sauce.
  6. Reduce heat, cover the pot with foil and a lid. Simmer for 30–35 minutes.
  7. Stir halfway through to prevent burning. Add a splash of water if needed.
  8. Once rice is tender and fluffy, remove from heat and serve hot.
